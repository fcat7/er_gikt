Calculation with + - * /, Order of Operations
Absolute Value, Square Root, Computation with Real Numbers
2. 数论基础 (Number Theory)
包含：因数、倍数、质数、科学计数法

Greatest Common Factor, Least Common Multiple
Prime Number, Divisibility Rules, Exponents, Scientific Notation
Properties of Numbers, Rounding, Estimation
3. 分数与比例 (Fractions & Proportions)
包含：分数运算、比例、单位比率

Fractions (Add/Sub/Mult/Div, Equivalent, Ordering)
Proportion, Ratio, Rate, Unit Rate, Finding fractions and ratios
4. 百分比应用 (Percentages)
包含：百分数计算、折扣、增长率

Percents, Finding Percents, Percent Of
Percent Discount, Percent Increase or Decrease
Conversion of Fraction Decimals Percents

包含：化简、代入、分配律

Algebraic Simplification, Combining Like Terms

包含：解方程、不等式求解

Linear Equations, Solving Systems of Linear Equations

Slope (Finding from Graph/Equation/Ordered Pairs/Situation)

8. 多项式与二次方程 (Polynomials & Quadratics)

Polynomials (Monomial, Factors, Multiplying)

Finding Max and Min from a Quadratic Equation
9. 平面几何基础 (Basic Geometry: Angles & Shapes)

Angles (Obtuse, Acute, Parallel Lines, Interior)

Quadrilaterals, Polygons, Geometric Definitions

Area (Circle, Triangle, Rectangle, Trapezoid, Irregular)

Volume (Cone, Cylinder, Prism, Sphere, Pyramid)
Surface Area (Cylinder, Prism, Sphere)
Nets of 3D Figures

Transformation, Translations, Reflection, Rotations, Symmetry

13. 统计分析 (Statistics)
